# How to Test the Teleprompter App on Your Phone (Super Simple Steps!)

## What You Need:
1. Your phone (iPhone or Android)
2. This computer
3. Both devices connected to the same WiFi network

---

## Step 1: Start the App Server (On Your Computer)

Open the terminal/command prompt on your computer and type:

```
npm start
```

You'll see text appearing in the terminal. Look for a **QR code** - it will look like a square with a bunch of black and white dots.

---

## Step 2: Get the Expo Go App (On Your Phone)

1. Open your phone's app store (App Store for iPhone, Play Store for Android)
2. Search for: **"Expo Go"**
3. Download and install it (it's free!)
4. Open the Expo Go app

---

## Step 3: Scan the QR Code (On Your Phone)

1. In the Expo Go app, look for a **camera icon** or **scan** button
2. Point your phone at the QR code on your computer screen
3. Scan it

**That's it!** The app will load on your phone. Wait about 10-20 seconds for it to appear.

---

## You Should Now See:

✅ **Teleprompter** app screen
✅ Text input box (where you paste your script)
✅ Font size slider
✅ Speed slider  
✅ Big green **PLAY** button

---

## How to Use:

1. **Type or paste your script** in the text box
2. **Adjust text size** if you want it bigger/smaller
3. **Adjust scroll speed** if you want it faster/slower
4. **Tap the PLAY button**
5. **Phone will rotate to landscape** (sideways)
6. **Text scrolls automatically** ← This is your teleprompter!
7. **Tap the screen** to show controls
8. **Tap the ORANGE Exit button** to go back

---

## Troubleshooting:

**"The app won't load"**
- Make sure both your computer and phone are on the **same WiFi network**
- Check that the terminal is still running (don't close it!)

**"QR code won't scan"**
- Hold your phone steady for 3 seconds
- Make sure the QR code is fully visible on your screen

**"The text won't scroll"**
- Make sure you typed or pasted something in the script box
- Press the PLAY button (it should turn red/pink when playing)

---

## Notes:

- While testing, your phone shows a live preview of the code from your computer
- Any changes you make to the code on your computer will automatically reload on your phone
- Once you're done testing, you can close the terminal to stop the server

**That's it! You're testing a real mobile app! 🎉**
